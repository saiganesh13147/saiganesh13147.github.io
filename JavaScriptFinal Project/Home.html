Indian Cinema

The cinema of India consists of films produced in the nation of India. Cinema is immensely popular in India, with as many as 2,000 films produced in various languages every year. Indian cinema produces more films watched by more people than any other country; in 2011, over 3.5 billion tickets were sold across India, 900,000 (0.03%) more than Hollywood.

Why is Indian Cinema becoming so popular?

Indian cinema is a global enterprise. Its films have a following throughout Southern Asia and across Europe, North America, Asia, the Greater Middle East, Eastern Africa, China and elsewhere, reaching in over 90 countries.

Indian Box Office Revenue

The gross box office realisations of Indian film industry are expected to reach US $3.7 billion by 2020, according to Deloitte Indywood Indian Film Industry Report. As per December 2015, the gross box office realisation was US $2.1 billion.
